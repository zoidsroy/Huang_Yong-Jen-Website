Biography

Huang Yong-Jen

  • New Media Art Creator . Music Producer . Creative Coder

Born in Kaohsiung in 1997, she graduated from the Department of Applied Music, Truth University in 2019, majoring in composition, and is currently studying at the Graduate School of New Media Art, National Taipei University of the Arts. She is currently studying at the Graduate School of New Media Arts, National Taipei University of the Arts. She has been thinking about Mauricio Kagel's new music theater and figurative music, and is trying to extend it to the development of internet culture and digital media in her creation. In 2020, he joined the XTRUX New Media Art team, where he is responsible for sound design and the development of game engines and interactive technologies.
